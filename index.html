Introduction

The objective of the Smart Fertilizer Management System is to provide farmers with an intelligent and automated solution for managing fertilizer application in their fields. The system aims to optimize fertilizer usage based on crop type, soil conditions, and nutrient levels


Smart Fertilizer Management System is designed to address the challenges associated with traditional fertilizer management practices. The system integrates technology to automate the process of determining the optimal amount and type of fertilizer required for a specific crop in a given soil condition. The use of a Full Stack approach involves both frontend and backend development, ensuring a seamless user experience and efficient data management.

Ministry of Agriculture has realized this and is committed to make its utmost contribution to achieve its mission of bringing about prosperity through providing necessary guidance and co-ordination activities for sustainable development in Agriculture.
